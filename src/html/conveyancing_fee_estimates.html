Conveyancing Fee Estimates

We offer fee estimates for a range of residential conveyancing and related services.  Due to unforeseen matters that can arise in the course of a conveyancing matter, we offer an estimate rather than a fixed fee.  But we always do our best to stick to the estimate.

Our fees estimates are exclusive of GST and disbursements. For convenience, we have provided an estimated invoice total that you might expect to pay at the conclusion of your matter. The estimated invoice total takes into account the typical disbursements we would incur on your behalf, such as title searches ($5 each) and registration charges ($80 each), office disbursement fee ($50), and GST. However, the estimated invoice total excludes mileage.
