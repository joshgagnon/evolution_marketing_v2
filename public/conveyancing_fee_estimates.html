Conveyancing Fee Estimates

We offer fee estimates for a range of residential conveyancing and related services.  Due to unforeseen matters that can arise in the course of a conveyancing matter, we offer an estimate rather than a fixed fee.  But we always do our best to stick to the estimate.

Our fees estimates are exclusive of GST and disbursements. For convenience, we have also provided an approximate invoice total that you might expect to pay at the conclusion of your matter. The approximate invoice amount takes into account the typical disbursements we would incur on your behalf, such as title searches ($5 each) and registration charges ($80 each), office disbursement fee ($25) and GST.

Refinance of residential property

Our fee estimate - $680 ($780 if property held in trust)

Approximate invoice total - $980 ($1,100 if property held in trust)

Increasing mortgage priority amount

Our fee estimate - $480

Approximate invoice total - $665

Sale of residential property

Our fee estimate - $780

Approximate invoice total - $1,000

Purchase of residential property

Our fee estimate - $880

Approximate invoice total - $1,200

Purchase of property ‘off the plans’

Our fee estimate - $1,500

Approximate invoice total - $2,000


We do not charge first home buyers extra for the attendances unique to their matters, such as making a KiwiSaver Withdrawal or HomeStart Grant application.

Refinance and transfer of property e.g. to a look-through company

Our fee estimate - $1,000

Approximate invoice total - $1,450

Pre-auction document review (if not successful at auction)

Our fee estimate - $250

Approximate invoice total - $300

Establishing a basic family trust

Our fee estimate - $500

Approximate invoice total - $600

Preparing a basic will

Our fee estimate - $250

Approximate invoice total - $300

Our estimates are based on the time usually required for the typical attendances involved in a matter of the various types set out above. In 86% of cases, based on our review in October 2016, we do not exceed the estimate. Not all matters are born equal, however, so occasionally more time is required.
