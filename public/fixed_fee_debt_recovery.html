Conditions of our fixed fee services:

- All fees exclude GST and disbursements.

- For simple debt claims over $15,000 involving a single creditor and debtor (claims up to $15,000, and $20,000 by agreement, can be heard in the Disputes Tribunal.

- Debt to be based on a standard written document or one prepared by a lawyer.

- The written document is signed by the debtor.

- Debts covered by the Construction Contracts Act 2002 are excluded.

- Debts must be due and owing, and undisputed by the debtor.

- Fees at each step assume that all prior steps (if any) have been completed.

- If any recovery step is defended by the debtor or the debtor engages counsel to act on their behalf, fees will be charged in accordance with our usual hourly rates.
